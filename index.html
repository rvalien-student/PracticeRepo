Richard's Top Elevator Pitches

Track My Workout Time App

For gym goers who want to track actual training time, the Track My Workout Time App is a cloud storage app that provides a web based interface to track and log "actual" exercise and rest time during a workout Unlike tracking your workout in a paper notebook and performing manual calculations, our product automatically calculates and stores your workout stats in the cloud, which are then accessible from anywhere a web connection is available.
