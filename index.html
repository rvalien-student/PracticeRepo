Richard's Top Elevator Pitches

Track My Workout Time App

For those gym goers who want to track actual training time, the Track My Workout Time App is a cloud storage app that provides a web based interface to track and log "actual" exercise and rest time during a workout. Unlike tracking your workout in a paper notebook and performing manual calculations, our product automatically calculates and stores your workout stats in the cloud, which are then accessible from anywhere a web connection is available.

Track My Gas App

For vehicle drivers who wish to log mileage and related expenses, the Track My Gas App is a cloud storage app that provides a web based interface to keep track of mileage, gas quantity, and price at each tank fill-up. Unlike logging gas expenses in a paper notebook and performing manual calculations, our product makes keeping track of mileage and related expenses easy by providing a web based interface to enter date, mileage, quantity and price at each fill-up. The app calculates MPG, total miles and gallons used for a user specified time period.

Grocery List App

For grocery shoppers who want to organize and easily maintain their grocery list in one place, the Grocery List App is a cloud storage app that provides an easy to use web based interface to read and maintain your list of grocery items. Unlike using a paper list which can be forgotten or misplaced, our product provides web access to your list from anywhere a web connection is available. The web interface allows ease of maintenance of your list by providing item name and category, along with add, delete, markup and sort operations.
